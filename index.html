Our History

Our team was created after several of us participated in hackathons and other competitions and realized that we work very well together, can deliver results, and we tend to win. So in March 2021 we decided to make our team more formal and incorporate it as a company, with the thinking that maybe one of the prototypes will at some point become products. And that is where we are today.
